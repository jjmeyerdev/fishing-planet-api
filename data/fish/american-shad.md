# American Shad

## How to catch

How to catch **a trophy American Shad**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch American Shad would be to use **Shrimp** on a size #3/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Grubs**. The most successful lure is **Purple/Black Club-Series Twin Tail Spider Grub 7 cm**.
