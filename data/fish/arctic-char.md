# Arctic Char

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Arctic Char**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Arctic Char would be to use **Finger Mullets** on a size #4/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Plugs**. The most successful lure is **Light Wood Plug 75 g, #6/0**.
