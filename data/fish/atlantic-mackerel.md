# Atlantic Mackerel

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Atlantic Mackerel**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Atlantic Mackerel would be to use **Finger Mullets** on a size #2/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Slop Spoons**. The most successful lure is **Gold Slop Spoon 14 g, #1/0**.
