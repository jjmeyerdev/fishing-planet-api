# Atlantic Salmon

## How to catch

How to catch **a trophy Atlantic Salmon**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Atlantic Salmon would be to use **Shiners** on a size #5/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Spinners**. The most successful lure is **Rainbow Spinner 14 g, #3/0**.
