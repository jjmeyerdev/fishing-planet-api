# Azov Shad

## How to catch

How to catch **a trophy Azov Shad**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Azov Shad would be to use **Mayflies** on a size #1 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Nano Spoons**. The most successful lure is **Green and Black Nano Spoon 6 g, #1/0**.
