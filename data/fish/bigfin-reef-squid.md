# Bigfin Reef Squid

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Bigfin Reef Squid**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Bigfin Reef Squid would be to use **Medium Fish Strips** on a size #6/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Squid Jigs**. The most successful lure is **Silver/Gold Trophy Squid Jig 15 g, #2/0**.
