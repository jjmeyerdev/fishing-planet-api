# Black Marlin

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Black Marlin**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Black Marlin would be to use **Large Flying Fish** on a size #10/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Trolling Octopuses**. The most successful lure is **Glowing Blue Glowing Trolling Octopus 230 g, #14/0**.
