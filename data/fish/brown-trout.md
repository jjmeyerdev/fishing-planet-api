# Brown Trout

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Brown Trout**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Brown Trout would be to use **Shiners** on a size #4/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Narrow Spoons**. The most successful lure is **Yellow/Black X-Series Barbless Spinner 14 g, #3/0**.
