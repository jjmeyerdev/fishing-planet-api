# Bull Shark

## How to catch

How to catch **a trophy Bull Shark**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Bull Shark would be to use **Blood** on a size #10/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Shads**. The most successful lure is **Glow White & Red Head Shad 15 cm**.
