# Channel Catfish

Ictalurus punctatus

## Description

Channel catfish (Ictalurus punctatus) is North America's most numerous catfish species and the most fished catfish species in the USA.

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Channel Catfish**?

When it comes to float, feeder, or bottom fishing, the best way to catch Channel Catfish would be to use **Large Minnows** on a size #4/0 hook.
