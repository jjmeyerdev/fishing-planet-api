# Cyber Piranha

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Cyber Piranha**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Cyber Piranha would be to use **Blood** on a size #4/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Swimbaits**. The most successful lure is **Natural Blue Duo Swimbait 56 g, #4/0**.
