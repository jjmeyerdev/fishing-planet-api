# Electric Eel

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Electric Eel**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Electric Eel would be to use **Amazonian Sardine** on a size #5/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Buzzbaits**. The most successful lure is **Acid Green and Orange Buzzbait 28 g, #5/0**.
