# European Chub

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique European Chub**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch European Chub would be to use **Small Minnows** on a size #1 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Walkers**. The most successful lure is **Natural Walker 14 g, #3/0**.
