# European Hake

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique European Hake**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch European Hake would be to use **Giant Tiger Prawn** on a size #10/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Fish Pilkers**. The most successful lure is **Silver/Yellow Sonar Fish Pilker 200 g, #4/0**.
