# Florida Gar

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Florida Gar**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Florida Gar would be to use **Shiners** on a size #4/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Frogs**. The most successful lure is **Silver Trophy Frog-popper 18 g, #3/0**.
