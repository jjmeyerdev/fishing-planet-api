# Freshwater Drum

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Freshwater Drum**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Freshwater Drum would be to use **Small Minnows** on a size #2/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Shads**. The most successful lure is **Yellow & Red Shad 10 cm**.
