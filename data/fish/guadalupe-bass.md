# Guadalupe Bass

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Guadalupe Bass**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Guadalupe Bass would be to use **Shiners** on a size #2/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Bass Jigs**. The most successful lure is **Orange Brown Pepper Bass Jig 14 g, #3/0**.
