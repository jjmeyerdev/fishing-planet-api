# Kamba Catfish

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Kamba Catfish**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Kamba Catfish would be to use **Shiners** on a size #10/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Shads**. The most successful lure is **Purple Dust Ribbed Shad 15 cm**.
