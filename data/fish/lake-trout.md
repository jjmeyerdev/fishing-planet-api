# Lake Trout

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Lake Trout**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Lake Trout would be to use **Large Minnows** on a size #4/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Medium Spoons**. The most successful lure is **Lemon Tiger Medium Spoon 42 g, #4/0**.
