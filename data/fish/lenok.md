# Lenok

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Lenok**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Lenok would be to use **Bark Beetle Larva** on a size #4/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Mouse Lures**. The most successful lure is **Black Mouse Lure 14 g, #2/0**.
