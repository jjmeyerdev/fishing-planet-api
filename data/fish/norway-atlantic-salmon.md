# Norway Atlantic Salmon

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Norway Atlantic Salmon**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Norway Atlantic Salmon would be to use **Lesser Sand Eel** on a size #9/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Plugs**. The most successful lure is **Silver/Gold Trophy Plug 115 g, #8/0**.
