# Payara

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Payara**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Payara would be to use **Shiners** on a size #4/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Poppers**. The most successful lure is **Blue & Silver Major Popper 42 g, #5/0**.
