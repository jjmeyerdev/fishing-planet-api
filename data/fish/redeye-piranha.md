# Redeye Piranha

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Redeye Piranha**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Redeye Piranha would be to use **Camu-Camu** on a size #5/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Swimbaits**. The most successful lure is **Acid Green and Orange Buzzbait 24 g, #4/0**.
