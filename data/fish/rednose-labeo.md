# Rednose Labeo

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Rednose Labeo**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Rednose Labeo would be to use **Sweet Peas** on a size #1/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Larvae**. The most successful lure is **Acid Green Larva 10 cm**.
