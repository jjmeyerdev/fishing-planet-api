# Sea Trout

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Sea Trout**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Sea Trout would be to use **Scaled Sardines** on a size #7/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Plugs**. The most successful lure is **Light Wood Plug 85 g, #8/0**.
