# Silver Arowana

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Silver Arowana**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Silver Arowana would be to use **Large Minnows** on a size #6/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Swimbaits**. The most successful lure is **Natural Blue Duo Swimbait 56 g, #4/0**.
