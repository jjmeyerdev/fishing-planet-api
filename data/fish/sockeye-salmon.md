# Sockeye Salmon

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Sockeye Salmon**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Sockeye Salmon would be to use **Shiners** on a size #4/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Bullet Spinners**. The most successful lure is **Black and Pink Single Spoon 42 g, #4/0**.
