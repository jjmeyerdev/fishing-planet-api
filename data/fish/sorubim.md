# Sorubim

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Sorubim**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Sorubim would be to use **Dead Lambari Fish** on a size #7/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Crayfish**. The most successful lure is **Christmas Jelly Crayfish 10 cm**.
