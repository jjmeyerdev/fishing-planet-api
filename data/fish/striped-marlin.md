# Striped Marlin

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Striped Marlin**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Striped Marlin would be to use **Japanese Jack Mackerel** on a size #10/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Plugs**. The most successful lure is **Light Wood Plug 190 g, #10/0**.
