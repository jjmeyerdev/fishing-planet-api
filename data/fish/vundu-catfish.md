# Vundu Catfish

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Vundu Catfish**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Vundu Catfish would be to use **Large Minnows** on a size #8/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Shads**. The most successful lure is **Yellow & Red Shad 15 cm**.
