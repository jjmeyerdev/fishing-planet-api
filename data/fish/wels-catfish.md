# Wels Catfish

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Wels Catfish**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Wels Catfish would be to use **Leeches** on a size #6/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Jerkbaits**. The most successful lure is **Dim Green / Glow Green Crankbait 9 m, #6/0**.
