# White Bass

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique White Bass**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch White Bass would be to use **Shrimp** on a size #4/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Narrow Spoons**. The most successful lure is **Green Holographic Narrow Spoon 14 g, #2/0**.
