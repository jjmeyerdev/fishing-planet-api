# Yellowfin Tuna

## How to catch

How to catch **a unique Yellowfin Tuna**? You can catch this fish either with **bait** or **lures**.

If you prefer to use natural baits, then the best way to catch Yellowfin Tuna would be to use **Indian Ocean Squid** on a size #10/0 hook.

If you are more into artificial lures, you should go for some **Trolling Octopuses**. The most successful lure is **Light Wood Plug 150 g, #10/0**.
